meyeADHD Device

Deep in the build of our protoytpe, we have developed a system for acquiring pupil data by RGB camera with IR bandwidth. We are processing this data using MATLAB's binary image converter and shape recognition functions. Post processing, we plan to determine an ideal sorting algorithm and determine which structural elements of the algorithm make it successful.

Our development depends on your feedback and interaction with our tool. We greatly appreciate your participation. Contact us by navigating to our team page and filling in your information or email us at debut@cornell.edu.
